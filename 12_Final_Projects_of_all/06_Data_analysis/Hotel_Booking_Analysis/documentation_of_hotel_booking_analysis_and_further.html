1. Problem Identification

Hotels lose massive revenue from last-minute cancellations. The objective was to build an AI system to predict is_canceled probability, enabling dynamic deposit requests.

The data patterns that drive our "Concierge" recommendations.

Lead Time vs Cancellation

PRIMARY RISK FACTOR

Analyst Note: As lead time increases (booking > 90 days out), the probability of cancellation rises sharply. This variable carries the highest weight in our XGBoost model.

Guest Intent

Special Requests

Insight: An inverse relationship. Guests who make requests (parking, high floor) show higher commitment.

Booking Split

City hotels have higher volume but also higher churn volatility compared to Resort hotels.

Top Origins

Geographic Risk

Insight: Portugal (PRT) and UK (GBR) are top markets. Domestic bookings (PRT) often have different cancellation behaviors than international ones.

Deposit Impact

Financial Security

Non-Refundable bookings are revenue-secured, allowing us to lower the "risk score" even if probability is high.

Customer Loyalty

RETENTION METRIC

Analyst Note: Repeated guests have a near-zero cancellation rate (approx 95% retention).

The system automatically tags these users as "VIP Safe", bypassing standard risk checks and offering express check-in.
