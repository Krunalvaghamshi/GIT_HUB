The Refinery: Data Wrangling

Leveraging pandas and Regex, we systematically dismantled the messy columns. We extracted pure integers from string soups, standardized global pricing to USD, and exploded nested lists into distinct feature vectors.

Currency Normalization

Established USD as the anchor currency. Eliminated localized formatting (commas, currency codes) to ensure numerical consistency for regression models.

Feature Explosion

Decomposed array-like structures [48MP, 12MP] into orthogonal features: Camera_Main, Camera_Tele, Camera_Ultra.

03

Visual Intelligence

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) reveals the hidden correlations and market stratification strategies. We observe a clear dichotomy between "spec-sheet warriors" and "brand-equity drivers."

Market Dominance

CATEGORICAL DIST

Analysis: The market shows a Pareto-like distribution where Samsung and Apple command nearly 40% of the dataset volume, indicating their broad product portfolio strategies compared to niche players.

Temporal Pricing

TIME SERIES

Analysis: Apple exhibits low volatility with high sustained pricing. Chinese OEMs (Xiaomi, Oppo) demonstrate aggressive upward pricing mobility, entering premium tiers in late 2024.

The Silicon Premium

MEDIAN AGGREGATION

A definitive hierarchy exists. Devices with A17 Bionic and Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 command a 200%+ price delta over mid-range silicon, confirming that the processor is the primary determinant of "Flagship" status.

RAM vs Price Correlation

SCATTER REGRESSION

Insight: Positive linear correlation (R² ≈ 0.7). However, diminishing returns are observed beyond 12GB RAM, where price variance increases significantly due to brand factors.

Hardware Density (Weight vs Battery)

ENGINEERING METRIC

Insight: Most devices cluster around 5000mAh/200g. Outliers represent "Rugged" phones (heavy, high capacity) or "Slim" flagships (light, lower capacity), revealing engineering trade-offs.

04

ML Readiness & Scaling

To prepare for high-dimensional regression models, we applied rigorous transformations. Raw numerical magnitude bias was eliminated, and categorical data was encoded into orthogonal vectors.

1

Label Encoding

Converted nominal categorical variables (Brand, Processor) into machine-readable ordinal integers.

Apple → 0, Samsung → 12, Xiaomi → 21
2

Min-Max Scaling

Normalized feature space to [0,1] interval. This prevents features with large magnitudes (Battery: 5000) from dominating gradients over small features (RAM: 8).
